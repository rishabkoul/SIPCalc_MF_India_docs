How It Works

  • Uses multi-timeframe analysis (7d, 15d, 30d, 90d)
  • Incorporates CAGR and market cycles
  • Adapts to market volatility
  • Provides risk-adjusted growth projections
  • Implements dynamic trend bounds

Key Features

  • Market cycle awareness
  • Volatility-based adjustments

  • Conservative long-term projections

Best Practices

  • Use at least 1 year of historical data
  • Consider market conditions
  • Monitor prediction confidence
  • Check is_predicted flag in responses
  • Review day-wise NAV progression

Example: Future Returns Calculation

curl --request POST \
  --url https://sipcalc-mf-india-api.p.rapidapi.com/calculate-returns \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --header 'x-rapidapi-host: sipcalc-mf-india-api.p.rapidapi.com' \
  --header 'x-rapidapi-key: your-rapidapi-key' \
  --data '{
    "scheme_code": "119598",
    "start_date": "2023-01-01", 
    "end_date": "2025-12-31",
    "amount": 5000,
    "frequency": "daily"
  }'
